ABOUT

DA POETRY LOUNGE

Over the last 23 years, DPL has grown to be an unparalleled force of artistic expression in Los Angeles, celebrating and advancing the culture through impactful and insightful prose. Co-founded by Shihan Van Clief and Dante Basco, DPL has been the inspiration for countless creations and imitations, including Def Poetry Jam which ran for 6 seasons on HBO, and has fostered a community that now spans the globe.
