IN THE LAB

At Solis Labs, we use blockchain (NFT & DeFi) technologies to FACILITATE the funding, development and monetization of content and the resulting IP for a generation of digital creators.

Through our relationships and expertise, we collectively COLLABORATE with our existing network of actors, producers, directors, musicians and artists to build IP that will INSPIRE a global community of dreamers to dream bigger than they ever have dared to dream before.

We utilize our token, our content, and our marketplace to CONNECT artists and a worldwide audience, democratizing the opportunity to INVEST in the content and the art that most appeals to them, thus launching a self sustaining ecosystem that allows the world to CREATE and participate on a scale like never before.

OUR MARKET

Solis’ NFT Marketplace provides highly sought Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) focused on access to exclusive and rare Hollywood content, experiences and memorabilia.

Our Marketplace will also provide ‘royalty-backed’ and fractionalized NFTs:

  • - Access to Solis’ revenues for both creators and audiences
  • - Greenlight and finance content
