Each of our Associate Members who participate in TIA’s Marketplace have made a substantial commitment to the industry they serve, as indicated by their Marketplace Shield, indicating their level of sponsorshp. The higher the level of sponsorship, the higher the level of commitment to providing TIA members with regular news, information, expertise and advice in the form of articles, podcasts, webinars, and presentations.
