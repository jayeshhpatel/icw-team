Hello, we’re Solis We

Empower Storytellers, Create Content And Communities

SOLIS is a multi-company, self-sustaining ecosystem of IP creation, distribution and monetization that utilizes its own token to organically connect Hollywood talent and curated content to the world through the first entertainment industry focused Web 3.0 decentralised NFT marketplace and token economy.

SOLIS creates and invests in content for film, television and SVOD and utilizes NFT (Non-fungible tokens) and decentralised finance blockchain technologies to connect creators and talent with content consumers through financing, reward and engagement programs.

Our Mission

Facilitate

SOLIS at its core is a revolutionary solution to establish innovative new models advancing intimate layers of engagement between creators, talent, audiences and funds, ultimately democratizing the creation and monetization of content designed to entertain and inspire a global audience.

Collaborate

We do this through a unique reimagining of how content is funded and generated, in combination with intimate access to a global community of the most influential and talented professionals the entertainment industry has to offer.

Inspire

Through our original methodologies, we will be the innovators in developing interactive mechanics to greenlight and generate content for screens of all sizes and audiences of all nations, instantly becoming a curated community of diversely powerful storytellers for a global audience.

Create

We create film, television, impact driven livestream content and SVOD (streaming video on demand) involving some of the most decorated and respected entertainment industry professionals and talent, spanning the global film, television and music scenes. We will continue to generate significant opportunities to participate in Hollywood productions through our diverse network of partnerships with key writers, directors, and production houses to support IP (intellectual property) that will amplify SOLIS’ visibility and success within the global market.

Invest

We will further foster and amplify the voices and creative talent on a global scale, maximizing production opportunities with domestic incentives while supporting all our storytellers through our relationships throughout Hollywood, ASIA and Australia. We facilitate these through investment in both SOLIS content and co-production (Executive Producer) with established and successful Hollywood studios.

Connect

We connect creators and talent (established and future) with global content consumers, both physically, as well as utilizing sophisticated NFT (non-fungible token) and decentralised finance (DeFi) blockchain technologies, as the digital conduit to investment, collaboration and engagement.

The SOLIS token forms the centre of Solis’ decentralised economy and revenue model.

Business Strategy

Our business strategy focuses on a three-pronged approach to maximize opportunities across the global entertainment industry both traditionally and through NFT & DeFi blockchain & Web 3.0 technologies.

Television + Live Streaming

HMBL is the division of SOLIS that develops and produces impact driven television, livestream content & SVOD.

HMBL House generates revenue from both traditional and decentralized content with a portfolio of acclaimed productions utilising some of the entertainment industry’s most decorated and respected talent and producers.

Defi + Nft Market

SOLIS Labs is the blockchain division and NFT marketplace for SOLIS’ content and for its entertainment partners and talent.

Solis’ Token forms the backbone of a digital suite that will allow creators and their communities to connect, develop, engage and participate in the content they care about most.

Feature Film Production

SOLIS Studios develops, procures and manages feature film investments with established and successful Hollywood production houses.

SOLIS Studios IP includes content creation, film development and Executive Producer investment, NFT sales, spin-offs, remakes, licensing deals, merchandise and all other forms of IP exploitation by the company.

Business Model

SOLIS generates revenue (current and future), both traditional and non-traditional, through;

  • Production management
  • Creative services
  • Monetization of created content (licensing, royalties) to distributors and publishers
  • New revenue streams for ancillary intellectual property (merchandise, spin-offs)
  • Sales of NFTs for SOLIS’ and Creators’ intellectual property and tangible assets via SOLIS NFT Market
  • Decentralized finance (DeFi) blockchain technologies within SOLIS’ token ecosystem via $SOLIS Token
  • Streaming revenue
  • Advertising revenue
  • Royalties from Solis and co-produced content
  • Transaction revenue within its marketplace and ecosystem

SOLIS LABS

A Premier NFT Marketplace

SOLIS.market is a decentralized NFT exchange ecosystem.

The SOLIS NFT marketplace is built on the Polygon blockchain, a layer-2 protocol offering superior scalability, low transaction costs and an equally developer and consumer-friendly environment.

Problems With Most Other NFT Platforms

Crowded with NFTs with little or no initial value or secondary market value, due to;

  • No celebrity/talent/production NFT value for the purchaser
  • Little appeal for high quality NFT Creators to use most platforms
    • No (or limited) secondary royalties
    • No platform quality control - lost in a sea of poor quality NFTs
  • Lack of scarcity and/or rarity to the digital or physical NFT
  • Disengaged user base on the platforms
  • No (or limited) access to physical memorabilia or collectables in the entertainment industry
  • Difficult to on-sell due to lack of users relevant to the NFT category/type
    • lack of liquidity in secondary markets
  • High transaction fees (if on Ethereum)

THE SOLIS ADVANTAGE

Solis presents a superior experience by offering the following benefits

  • High appeal and valuable NFTs minted from A-list content (Film, Television, SVOD & Music productions, Talent, Celebrities, Producers, Artists, Musicians etc)
  • SOLIS owns existing content and is continually creating new content, partnerships and relationships
  • Perpetual secondary NFT sales revenue to Creators
  • Engage to Earn (E2E) within the SOLIS token economy
  • Buy, sell, mint (curated), stake, store – all within the one ecosystem
  • User base (members and stakers) specifically for SOLIS type content and experiences providing for liquidity and value driving in the secondary sales market
  • SOLIS provides both digital and physical NFT opportunities
  • SOLIS provides “experiences” in the entertainment industry via NFTs
  • SOLIS has relationships with charitable and philanthropic organisations
  • SOLIS token and ecosystem appeal, value drivers and functionality extends beyond its NFT marketplace

BUY BACK AND BURN POLICY

SOLIS will use random amounts of its group revenue (fiat earnings) to buy-back $SOLIS Token from exchanges and burn $SOLIS tokens, thereby decreasing the circulating supply. SOLIS will notify the market when buy back and burn events occur.

STAKING SOLIS

SOLIS economy will provide incentives to hold $SOLIS through various staking programs as well as driving the demand for the utility of $SOLIS across its network of content creators, producers, publishers and $SOLIS’ user base; all using the SOLIS platform (SOLIS’ NFT market and Wallet) to benefit from the ecosystem.
